What is CodeRush 2022?

Magnam dolores commodi suscipit eius consequatur

CodeRush is a team-based competition launched by INTECS for the fifth consecutive time. It is an ideal platform where you get to discover your skills in coding and problem-solving. During this two-day hackathon, your goal is to gather your team and brainstorm to untangle the solutions for the enigmas presented to you. Day 01 of the competition will take place on University premises while day 02 will be held online.

The competition comprises 3 rounds. The coding round of the competition would be conducted with the support of the HackerRank platform. In the idea submission round, the teams have to come up with an innovative solution to a given scenario. Last but not least there will be a selfie challenge and voting that will be a fun experience for our competitors. This mesmerizing event is exclusive to the members of the FIT family. So don't pass up this wonderful opportunity.

May the best team win!

Rules & Regulations

  • CodeRush 2022 is exclusively for the undergraduates of the Faculty of Information Technology, University of Moratuwa
  • The competition will be in action for two days, in both physical and virtual settings
  • A team can only log into the HackerRank platform by using the account and password sent by the Organizing Committee
  • The competition consists of HackerRank questions ranging from easy to medium and hard and also with an idea submission
  • The points earned through HackerRank will be accounted for 70% of total marks while 30% will be calculated through the marks earned for the Ideathon
  • The ranking of teams is done in accordance with the number of problems solved and the time taken for each
  • A team should consist of 4 members and a participant can be a member of one team only
  • The Contest Committee will be publishing team marks and standings at the end of each round
  • The Contest Committee's decisions on all matters will be final
  • Any violation of rules and regulations committed before or during the contest will result in the team towards ineligibility for the final prizes
  • Contact the Organizing Committee of CodeRush 2022 for further information
